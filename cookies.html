Cookies Policy

1. Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience while using our platform.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us improve functionality, analyze usage, and offer a better browsing experience.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:
- Ensure the website operates smoothly and efficiently.
- Personalize your experience and preferences.
- Analyze user behavior to enhance performance.
- Provide relevant promotions and advertisements.

4. Types of Cookies We Use

- Essential Cookies: Required for the proper functioning of the website.
- Performance Cookies: Used to collect data on how visitors use the site.
- Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings.
- Advertising Cookies: Deliver targeted ads based on your activity.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality and user experience of the site.

6. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our site may be provided by trusted third-party services for analytics, advertisements, or functionality enhancements.
